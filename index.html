Paris, Ile-De-France, 75007, France | 808. 624. 7725 | h.vo@architecture.com

An integrated architecture, design, planning, and consulting firm with roughly 15 professionals based in Paris, France.

The leading architect and founder is Henry Gilbert Volier. He is a licensed architect with a B.Arch from Cal Poly San Luis Obispo, and an M.Arch from the University of London. Being in the field for 12 years, his passion for unique, integrated, and problem solving design technics has led to the establishment of H.Vo. He is a British American based in Paris, France. The firm focuses on international clients from the United States and other parts of the EU. We are a small respectable firm established in 2011 with 25 employees and growing. Our focus is residential and commercial spaces. The main image the firm stands by is a creative, passionate, and an approachable style.
